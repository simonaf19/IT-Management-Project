**c) Phases** 

1. **Planning (2 months):** Development of Housing Quality Standards and certification framework.

2. **Preparation (3 months):** Organizational setup, inspector training preparation, and registry setup.

3. **Pilot implementation (5 months):** Training inspectors, launching the registry, and conducting initial inspections.

4. **Evaluation (2 months):** Review of results, user feedback, and adjustments.

5. **Public launch and expansion planning (2 months):** Official launch and preparation for expansion.

**d) Milestones**

1. Housing Quality Standard approved – April 2026

2. Online registry launched – January 2027

3. First certified apartment published – January 2027

4. Pilot phase completed – June 2027

## **PROJECT MANAGER AND TEAM, ROLES AND EMPLOYMENT** 

## **Project Manager (part-time, 12 months)** Responsible for organizing the project, managing the timeline and budget, coordinating partners, and preparing reports.

**Technical Lead (contract-based)**  
Responsible for developing and maintaining the online registry system.

**Inspection Coordinator (part-time)**  
Responsible for organizing inspector training and scheduling property inspections.

**Legal Advisor (external consultant)**  
Provides legal support related to certification rules, liability, and data protection.

**Steering Committee (internal members)**  
Provides supervision and approves key project decisions. Members participate as part of their regular university roles and are not additionally paid.

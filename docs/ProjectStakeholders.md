## **5\. Students (Tenants / End Users)**

**Status:** 

Positive

**Expectations / Interests:**  
Students want safe, reliable, and affordable housing. They expect clear and transparent information about property conditions, certifications, inspections, and complaints so they can make informed decisions easily. They also prefer simple summaries rather than complex reports.

**Possible Conflicts / Benefits:**

* **Benefit:** Increased safety, better housing choices, and reduced risk of renting unsafe properties  
* **Conflict:** Possible confusion or distrust if the system is unclear, too complex, or provides inaccurate/incomplete information

**Team Response:**

* Design a simple and user-friendly interface with clear safety indicators  
* Provide summarized information (e.g., certification status, risk level)  
* Ensure data accuracy and transparency  
* Include reviews and complaint visibility to support decision-making

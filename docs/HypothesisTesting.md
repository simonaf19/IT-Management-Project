# **Parent of a Student Tenant**

**Person interviewed:**  
Age: 50  
Role: Parent of a university student  
Background: Her daughter is studying abroad, and they are looking for a new apartment.

## **Questions and Answers**

**1\. How do you usually search for rental housing for your child?**  
 I usually search through online rental platforms and sometimes Facebook groups for students. I also look at Google Maps and reviews to see the location and the surrounding area.

**2\. What information is most important for you when choosing an apartment?**  
Safety is the most important. I want to know that the apartment is in good condition, that the building is safe, and that there are no serious issues like mold, humidity, or broken utilities.

**3\. Have you ever had concerns about the condition of an apartment before renting it?**  
Yes, many times the photos online look much better than the real apartment. It is difficult to know the real condition without visiting the place.

**4\. Would it help you if you could see official inspection or certification information about the apartment?**  
Yes, that would be very helpful. If I could see that the apartment had passed an inspection or had a safety certification, I would feel much more comfortable letting my child live there.

**5\. How important are reviews from previous tenants?**  
They are very important because they give a real perspective about the apartment and the landlord. I usually read several reviews before trusting a listing.

**6\. Would you use a system that shows inspections, complaints, and certifications for rental housing?**  
Yes, definitely. As a parent, I would prefer to rent a certified apartment because it gives me more confidence that the housing is safe and properly maintained.

## **Conclusion**

This interview supports the idea that **parents who help their children find housing also need reliable and transparent information about apartment safety and condition**. A platform that provides **inspection records, certifications, and tenant feedback** would help parents make safer decisions when choosing housing for their children.

**Possible Improvement Suggestions (Parent Perspective)**

* Add a **clear certification badge** so parents can quickly see if the apartment is safe.

* Provide a **short safety summary** instead of only detailed inspection reports.

* Include **verified tenant reviews** to make feedback more trustworthy.

* Show **complaint history** so parents can identify potential problems with the property.

# **International Student (Tenant)**

**Person interviewed:**  
 Age: 20  
 Role: International student renting housing  
 Background: Moved to Slovenia for university and is looking for affordable housing.

## **Questions and Answers**

1. **How did you find your current apartment?**  
   Through [MojCimer.si](http://MojCimer.si) website (student housing).  

2. **What difficulties did you experience while searching for housing in a new country?**  
   The main problem was the high prices and the lack of small one-room apartments.  

3. **Did you worry about hidden problems in the apartment (mold, humidity, safety issues)?**  
   Not really, I did not worry much about that before moving in.  

4. **What information would help you feel more confident before signing a rental contract?**  
   Knowing that the landlord is reliable and reasonable would make me feel more confident.  

5. **How important are reviews from previous tenants when choosing housing?**  
   They are very important because they show the real experience of people who lived there before.  

6. **Would you use a platform that shows official inspection results and housing certifications?**  
   Yes, I would use it to better understand the real condition of the apartment.  

7. **Would you be willing to pay slightly more for a certified and verified apartment?**  
   No, as a student, my budget is limited, so price is still a very important factor.

## **Conclusion**

This interview shows that students mainly focus on **price and availability** when searching for housing. **Reviews from previous tenants are very important**, since they provide real experiences about the property and the landlord. Students would likely use a platform showing **official inspections and certifications**, but they may not be willing to pay higher rent for certified apartments.

### **Possible Improvement Suggestions (Student Perspective)**

* Ensure that the platform remains **free and accessible for tenants**, since students often have a limited budget.

* Highlight **tenant reviews and experiences**, as students rely on peer feedback.

* Provide **simple and clear information** about housing inspections and certifications.

* Include **filters for price and apartment type** (e.g., one-room apartments) to help students find affordable options faster.



**Housing Quality & Safety Certification Program**  
 *A pilot initiative to standardize rental housing safety and transparency for students.*

## *Usability Testing*

**March 2026**  
**Authors:** Anastasija Simunoska 89231023, Simona Filiposka 89231165, Zhasmin Sarvarova 89231228, Amir Garipov 89231145


## **Participant – Student**

**Profile:** 20-year-old international student

| Task | Success | Time | Issues |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Find a property and check if it is safe | Yes | 35s | Focused only on price and location at first |
| View certification status and inspection history | No | 1.5 min | Did not understand the certification status |
| Check reviews and complaints | Yes | 45s | None |
| Identify if the property has any risks | Yes | 1min | Relied on reviews instead of inspection data |

**Observations:**

* The participant focused mainly on price and availability

* Reviews were the primary source of trust

* Certification was mostly ignored or misunderstood

**Issues Observed:**

* Inspection data was not engaging for the user

* The participant preferred quick, simple indicators instead of detailed information
